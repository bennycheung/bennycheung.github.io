Benny is a full stack architect and software engineer with more than 30 years of programming and application development experience. He is well-versed in multiple languages and has advanced skills in blockchain architecture, machine learning and enterprise application development and architecture. Benny has a deep understanding of geographic systems, medical insurance, financial and AI technologies. Benny led architecture engineering for many profitable commercial products and led development on web, API, and data analytics projects in the medical, software, geographic, and financial services sectors. Benny has a B.Sc. and M. Sc in Computer Science from the University of Guelph, where his master thesis is on AI's expert system technologies.
